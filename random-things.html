RANDOM THINGS

NCT

I like this group from Korea named NCT. Roughly explaining the concept of this group, NCT is a K-pop group under SM Entertainment. NCT stands for Neo Culture Technology, and the idea is that they're a group of infinite members who are constantly shifting into different sub-units in different cities around the world. Currently, there are 4 existing groups under NCT. Currently of the year of 2022, NCT is made up of 23 members. In order of their debut date is NCT U, NCT 127, NCT Dream and WayV. The first NCT unit that debuted is NCT U in April 2016. Next followed by NCT 127 in July 2016, NCT Dream in August 2016 and WayV debuted in January 2019. In 2018, 2020 and 2021, this group have a project called NCT2018, NCT2020 and NCT2021 for each respective year. Each project consists of every members of NCT that is currenty active that year. I started stanning them since 2018. Their fandom color is Neon Green.

Seventeen

Next to NCT, I also like another group called SEVENTEEN or SVT. SVT is a 13 member boy group under Pledis Ent but currently under Hybe Ent. They officially debuted on May 26, 2015. Their group name Seventeen is because they have 13 members + 3 units + 1 group = 17. They have 3 units which are the Vocal Team, HipHop Team, and Performance Team. They are known as a self-producing idol group as they are involved in composition and production of their own discography and choreography. Their fandom color is Rose Quartz and Serenity.
